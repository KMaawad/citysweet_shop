About Us

City Sweet - Kferhata, Zgharta On December 14, 2006, City Sweet opened its doors in Kferhata, Zgharta, bringing a fresh and vibrant take on dessert culture to the area. Known for its wide variety of high-quality sweets, this cozy shop quickly became a favorite spot for locals and visitors alike.

City Sweet offers an irresistible selection of traditional Lebanese treats like knefeh, awamat, and znoud el sett, alongside modern delights such as macarons and baklavas Their innovative twists on classic flavors and pistachio éclairs, have earned them rave reviews. With its welcoming atmosphere and passion for sweet creations, City Sweet is the go-to destination for anyone craving a little indulgence in Kferhata.
